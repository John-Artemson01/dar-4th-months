Happy 4th Monthsary.

I never thought a day like this would come into my life — a day where I look forward to tomorrow because I know I get to see you again. Every single day, I find myself longing for you. Even the smallest misunderstanding between us breaks me, because I could never truly be mad at you. I never want to be the reason there are tears in your eyes… and yet sometimes, the very thing I fear the most is what I end up doing. For that, I am truly sorry.

I am always thankful that I met you — an unexpected person who brought so much joy into my life. Every moment with you is the greatest gift I have ever received. You help me forget the things I struggle with, and you give me the strength to keep moving forward… toward a better future, hopefully with you in it.

I’m not asking for you to feel the same way that I do, but please know that I will always be here for you. I will support you in every way I can. Please don’t be too hard on yourself — I’m here, baby. You can always count on me.

I love you, always…
